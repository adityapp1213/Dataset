The government believes that by implementing these regulations, they can reduce the country's dependence on fossil fuels and mitigate the effects of climate change.

To comply with the new fuel efficiency regulations, automakers will have to invest in more fuel-efficient engines and technologies. This will require a significant amount of research and development, as well as the development of alternative fuel sources. The automotive industry has been working tirelessly to develop innovative solutions to meet these new regulations and reduce their carbon footprint.

In order to achieve fuel efficiency, automakers are exploring various technologies such as electric and hybrid vehicles. Electric vehicles run on electricity stored in batteries, which can be recharged using renewable energy sources such as solar or wind power. Hybrid vehicles, on the other hand, use a combination of an internal combustion engine and an electric motor, resulting in improved fuel efficiency and reduced emissions.
